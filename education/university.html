UiTM KEDAH

Mr. Froggy Introduction


UiTM Cawangan Kedah is a branch of Universiti Teknologi MARA established in Kedah, Malaysia. It is administered by a Rector and is responsible for a UiTM campus in Kedah, located in Sungai Petani.


UiTM Kedah was officially inaugurated on October 1, 1997, by YB Tun Daim Zainuddin, the Minister of Finance. It began its first operation in November 1997 with 162 students and 25 administrative staff.


The campus spans an area of 350 acres in Mukim Bujang (between Semeling and Merbok), located approximately 14 kilometers from Sungai Petani. It was developed based on the approval of Malaysia's 6th Budget Plan with a total amount of RM38.4 million.
